# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want `dscan` to be a welcoming, friendly project for everyone. We as
contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation a respectful and
harassment-free experience for all, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

We expect everyone to:

- Be kind, respectful, and considerate in all interactions.
- Welcome differing viewpoints and give/accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.
- Take responsibility for mistakes and learn from them.

Behavior that harms, harasses, demeans, or excludes others is not tolerated.

## Adoption

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1**
as its full Code of Conduct. Please read it for the complete standards,
responsibilities, scope, and enforcement guidelines.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the maintainer
at **mina.sameh.lameh@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and handled
confidentially and fairly, following the enforcement guidelines in the
Contributor Covenant 2.1.
